StudyBuddy

Group study app for for user convienience

Download App Here

StudyBuddy is a group study app the allows students of any age range to form study groups and learn together without having to find a designated location. Study Buddy lets students get in either a voice of video call with their class mates and and be able to study with eachother directly through the app; whether that be through using the built in flash card quiz or sharing their screen so that the other student(s) in the call can see what they are looking at. The goal of the app is to bring students together using the convienience of technology.

StudyBuddy Main features

In Real Time Video Chat

Ability to Share Screens

Flash Card Quizes
Be able to Chat Face to Face with your classmates without having to leave the comfort and convienince of your room.
Be able to share your screen with your classmate so that you both can see the same thing in real time, without having to describe anything to eachother.
You will have the ability to quiz your classmates and friends while in a call together so that you all can learn together easily and comfortably.

The Design and Development Process for StudyBuddy

I interviewed my girlfriends study group that her and her friends hold at least once a week in the library on NKU's campus. Here is wha they had to say...

What is the worst part of being in a study group?
"Having to drive there and back"

Would it be more convienient to be able to study with eachother from your home?
"So much easier especially since i dont have to get fully dressed up to go out in public and also would save us gas money too"

How would this be easier than simply facetiming a friend and studying that way?
"Well if it is all in one program then the group would be able to see the same thing at the same time instead of having to explain what you are seeing to the other person."

StudyBuddy's main goal is to bring students together and helping them improve their grades by using the convienienve of technology.

The way I came up with the idea of StudyBuddy was because one day I was went to the library to complete some of my own studies and as I was walking through there was a group at almost every table. Another thing that I noticed was that almost every student was using a laptop or some sort of device to help them study. Then the thought hit me, "Why do we come to the library to study with people just to sit on our laptop the whole time?" We should be able to study together without having to meet up in person.
